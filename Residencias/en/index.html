Changes of residence, for work, family or social reasons are becoming more common. Balms Abogados and Tramiuris, S.L., management and processing company, have specialised professionals who can help and advise you when applying for residency in Spain or even Spanish nationality. For this, taking into account the particular needs of each client and based on the provisions of Organic Law 4/2001, of January 11, on the Law and Freedoms of Foreigners in Spain and their integration, it carries out a detailed study in order to always choose the procedure that is most convenient for the client.

The services offered on immigration issues could be divided into two groups: residence permits and processing of Spanish nationality.
