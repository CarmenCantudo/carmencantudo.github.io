Commercial processing


Commercial operations require a series of unavoidable legal and bureaucratic procedures, which are usually complicated and require specialised attention. Tramiuris makes available to its clients its commercial processing area to avoid any administrative problems when undertaking a new professional project or to define or modify a company or commercial situation.

Among the services offered are:

  • Information notes about the corporate situation.
  • Application for negative certificate of the company name.
  • Elaboration of budgets and annual accounts that are sent with the convening of the annual meeting.
  • Publications in the BORME (Official Gazette of the Commercial Registry).
  • Company incorporation.
  • Capital increase and reduction.
  • Liquidation / dissolution of corporation.
  • Corporate resolutions.
  • Sole shareholder statements.
  • Appointment and removal of administrators, liquidators and auditors.
  • Powers.
  • Mergers and divisions.
  • Judicial or administrative orders.
  • Presentation of annual accounts and ledgers.
