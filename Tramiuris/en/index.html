To meet all the client needs with a personalised treatment is the priority objective of Balms Abogados. Therefore, in 1999, as a complement to legal, employment and tax advice, Balms Abogados offers to Tramiuris clients an administration, processing and immigration department to centralise through our team any procedure that the client may desire and avoid decentralisation of work and unnecessary travel.

Faithful to the professional philosophy of the firm, consisting of providing personalised services, management is aimed at both customers (Spanish or foreign) who need to hire the services of an agency as well as small and medium business. Tramiuris handles all bureaucratic procedures offering the client a guarantee and tranquillity backed by the seriousness and experience of professionals who compose it.
