If the purchase of shares was made in conjunction with the bank's own financing and pledge of shares


If the purchase of shares was made in jointly funded by the bank itself and pledging of shares, you can claim the annullability of both the purchase of shares and the loan agreements with the bank. The legal action is based on the existence of a defect invalidating the legal business for the existence of error that affected the contractual consent provided in the contract with the bank. The annullability of the legal business subscription of the shares entails the nullity of the loan agreement and the reimbursement of the sums unduly paid. This action has a limitation period of four years. In Spain, we had the precedent in a similar case of Banco Banesto.
