How much can I claim?

If you participated in the capital increase, you can claim all the money invested in the shares, plus the legal interest and the costs of the claim. The costs include attorney and attorney fees.


 
What is the cost of the claim?

The claim will be made to success, so you do not have to pay professional fees to file the claim. Our Firm will only charge a percentage of the money received from Banco Popular through the Judgment. Please contact us and we will inform you in detail about this.


 
Why claim?

In recent years there has been a great deal of development in the area of claims against financial institutions, which is fundamentally supported by Community legislation, in order to curb bank malpractice and strengthen the position of consumers. In this regard, it is worth noting the complaints regarding preferential shareholdings, the floor clause, the clause of imputation of mortgage expenses and in the case of Bankia shares. This last issue can be a reference for the claims against Banco Popular, although there are differences between the two. In our case, as in Bankia, shares of the entity were acquired based on financial information that was not in line with the reality of the entity. It should be remembered that between the capital increase of May 2016 and the purchase by Banco Santander for € 1 has only been a year. Therefore, it appears that the information given in the prospectus was not accurate and there have already been two audits that have confirmed this. Therefore, we recommend to start the judicial claim now to recover your money, since the chances of success are high.
